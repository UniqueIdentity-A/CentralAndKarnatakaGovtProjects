## Hidden Scheme Signals — Detection Criteria

A scheme qualifies as "Hidden" if 3+ of these are true:
1. Not listed on india.gov.in/schemes main page
2. No Wikipedia article
3. Google search returns < 5,000 results
4. No coverage in major news outlets in past 2 years
5. Applicant count < 10,000 for eligible population > 1 lakh
6. Only accessible via ministry subdomain or state department website
7. Benefits are significant (>₹10,000) but awareness is low
8. Targets a niche population (artisans of specific craft, specific district)

### Example Hidden Scheme Categories to Search For:
- Transgender welfare schemes (state and central)
- Schemes for specific craft traditions (e.g., Channapatna toys, Kasuti embroidery)
- Soil health / Farmer credit sub-schemes with additional benefits
- Insurance schemes bundled with banking products
- Schemes for migrant workers in Karnataka
- Scholar fellowships for minority communities
- Schemes for persons released from prison
- Border area development grants
- Schemes for fishermen in specific Karnataka coastal districts
